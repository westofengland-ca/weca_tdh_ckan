{{ _('About Transport Data Hub') }}

One of the key published goals of the Future Transport Zone initiative is to "Enable better public transport planning by using data to improve decision-making".

TDH aims to deliver a region-wide data platform to improve transport planning and operation, improve collaboration between Unitary Authorities (UAs) in the region, and support innovation.

West of England Combined Authority: https://www.westofengland-ca.gov.uk/
FTZ: https://www.westofengland-ca.gov.uk/what-we-do/transport/future-transport-zone
