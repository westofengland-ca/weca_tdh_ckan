The Transport Data Hub is the first comprehensive regional transport data catalogue for the South West of England. The Data Hub makes it possible for the transport community to find, share and add data with ease. It supports combining multiple datasets and by using powerful analytical and reporting functionality, can present the data in usable and accessible formats. This fosters collaboration and allows for greater confidence and new opportunities in data-driven plans, policies and decisions.

The data catalogue is a centralised inventory of data assets developed by the West Of England Combined Authority as part of the wider Future Transport Zone initiative.
